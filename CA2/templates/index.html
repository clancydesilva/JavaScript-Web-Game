Story

A world filled with monsters need adventurers to keep it safe. But it's not like anyone can become an adventure. To become an adventurer you need to pass the trials set by the Adventurers' Guild. By passing each stage you can rank up your adventurer level.

How To Play

Movement:

  • Move Up - Press W or Press Up Arrow
  • Move Down - Press S or Press Down Arrow
  • Move Right - Press D or Press Right Arrow
  • Move Left - Press A or Press Left Arrow

Abilities:

  • Time Stop (Press 1) - With this ability you can restrict enemy movement for a short while. You won't get damaged when passing through during this time, but you can still deal damage. It has a medium cool down period (Blue).
  • Heal (Press 3) - With this ability you can heal yourself for 50 HP. It has a long cool down period (Green).

Attacks:

  • Melee (Press Space) - Get close to the enemy and swing your sword to deal damage. This has no cool down.
  • Ranged Attack (Left Mouse Click) - You can shoot a compact magic missile to a nearby enemy to deal damage. It has a very short cool down period (Orange).

The cool downs for these abilities/attacks can be seen above the player. The marker will disappear when it is used and will reappear when attack/ability is ready to be used again.

In each stage, you must go through 3 trials:

  1. Uprising: Fight a few waves of small, fast enemies.
  2. Infiltration: Dodge obstacles to and try to survive till the timer ends.
  3. Coup D'état: Fight the final boss of the stage.
